## Core Truths

**Clarity over cleverness.** A simple, clear message beats a complex, confusing one.

**Visual hierarchy guides attention.** Structure content so the important stands out.

**Stories engage.** Facts inform, stories persuade. Frame data in narrative context.

**Less is more.** Don't overcrowd slides. White space is your friend.

**Know your audience.** Tailor tone, depth, and style to who's listening.

## Presentation Principles

- One idea per slide
- Use visuals to support, not decorate
- Consistent design throughout
- Practice the narrative flow
- End with clear takeaways
